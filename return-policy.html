Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: June 2023

Thank you for shopping at Blobby's Digital Store. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help as best we can. Please read this policy carefully.

Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales are generally final once the product has been downloaded or accessed. Digital products cannot be returned, exchanged, or refunded due to their immediate delivery and inability to be "returned" in a traditional sense.

By purchasing digital products from Blobby's Digital Store, you acknowledge that you understand and agree to this no-refund policy for downloaded digital items.

Exceptional Cases

However, I understand that issues can arise. Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • The digital product is demonstrably broken or corrupted and cannot be opened/used.
  • You were charged multiple times for the same product due to a technical error.
  • You received the wrong product entirely (different from what was advertised).
  • The product is significantly different from what was described in the listing.

In these cases, please contact me immediately with details of the issue.

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund based on the exceptional cases outlined above, please follow these steps:

  1. 1
    Email support@blobbydigital.com with the subject line "Refund Request - [Order Number]"
  2. 2
    Include your proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt)
  3. 3
    Provide a detailed explanation of the issue you're experiencing
  4. 4
    If applicable, include screenshots or other evidence demonstrating the problem
  5. 5
    Specify your preferred refund method (original payment method is standard)

All refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of purchase. Requests submitted after this period may not be considered.

Refund Timeline

Once your refund request is received and reviewed, I will respond within 3-5 business days. If your refund is approved:

Processing

The refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation when the refund has been processed.

Completion

Depending on your payment provider, it may take an additional 2-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.
